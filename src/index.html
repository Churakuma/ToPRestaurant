Masion de la Côte

In the heart of Paris, Maison de la Côte is a contemporary French fine-dining restaurant, led by Chef Pierre Leclerc. The menu is inspired by the rich culinary traditions of France, and the restaurant's ethos is rooted in sustainability and seasonality.

The bar serves a selection of classic and innovative cocktails, as well as a variety of spirits, wines and beers.

The dining room offers an elegant, comfortable space for guests to enjoy a meal, with a view of the open kitchen.

The terrace provides an outdoor dining experience, offering a view of the Eiffel Tower and the surrounding neighbourhood.
